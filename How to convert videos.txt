1. Open video in vlc, and press red sqaure to abort playback
2. go to Medien -> convertieren
3. select video
4. choose profile: video-h.264 + mp3 (mp4)
5. go to settings of profile and select Verkapselung: AVI
6. select audiocodec and choose MP3 at 48000 Hz
7. convert
8. when vlc asks to override file, close vlc by killing it in the task manager.
